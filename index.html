About Me

Philhis is an e-learning platform that uses augmented reality (AR) to bring Philippine history to life. It is designed for teachers and students who want to explore Philippine history in an engaging, interactive, and immersive way. With Philhis, you can scan markers using your smartphone or tablet to reveal AR content related to Philippine history, including virtual objects, historical figures, and immersive environments. Philhis covers a wide range of topics related to Philippine history, from the pre-colonial era to the modern era. It is designed to be used in the classroom or for individual learners.
